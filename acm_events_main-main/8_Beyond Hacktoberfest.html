Beyond Hacktoberfest

ACM IGDTUW, in collaboration with Lean In @IGDTUW, conducted the second session of the OPEN SOURCE SERIES on Beyond Hacktoberfest, on 24th October 2020. Session was conducted by Iti Shree, a final year student at IGDTUW, GSoC' 18 scholar at Apertus Association and a former intern at BNY Mellon, along with Medha Bhattacharya, a pre-final year student at IGDTUW, a GSoC' 20 scholar at National Resource for Network Biologyand and was also a part of Microsoft Codess 2020.The session introduced the students to GSoC: Google Summer of Code, which is an international annual program in which students complete open-source software coding project during the summer. Participants had an interactive Q&A with the speakers.
