ACM summer workshop on DS and ML -Expert lecture by Dr Chandra Prakash

The session was conducted in the Summer Workshop - 2020. The speaker was none other than Dr. Chandra Prakash. The session was on Reinforcement Learning. This was an interactive session and the students had one to one interaction with the Professor. A wide number of students participated in the session and all of them were provided with learning resources. The young minds shared their ideas on Reinforcement Learning. Attendees extolled the session and showed their willingness to venture into the field of Reinforcement Learning. The session was a big hit !
