ACM Summer Workshop on DS and ML - Expert Lecture by Mr. Rakesh Dubbudu

The session was conducted in the Summer Workshop - 2020. The speaker of the session was Mr. Rakesh Dubbudu, founder of Factly and Tedx Speaker. It was a session on Fake News. The participants were introduced about fake news and how it is spread. The main purpose of this session was to help the participants identify fake news in real life and how to tackle them. This session helped in awareness about fake news, and the attendees participated enthusiastically in the workshop.
