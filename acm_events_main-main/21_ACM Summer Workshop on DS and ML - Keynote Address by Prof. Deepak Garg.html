ACM Summer Workshop on DS and ML - Keynote Address by Prof. Deepak Garg

ACM - IGDTUW facilitated students with one-to-one interaction with Professor Deepak Garg, Director LeadingIndia.ai. This session was under the Summer Workshop - 2020. Prof Garg talked about how students may get projects and uplift their skills in ML and DL from the Leading India AI initiative. The students were provided with a step-by-step roadmap of internships and project building in ML and AI with the help of the Leading India AI website.
