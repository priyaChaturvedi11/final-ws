Advaya 2020-Ready.Set.Research - session by Dr Rishabh Kaushal

ACM IGDTUW conducted its week-long annual technical fest in online mode from 12th -19th April 2020. On 14th April 2020, an insightful webinar “Ready.Set.Research!” was conducted from 6 pm to 7:30 pm. Dr Rishabh Kaushal was the keynote speaker for the session. He is an assistant professor at IGDTUW. Apart from having an excellent academic graph, Dr Rishabh has presented papers in cities like Oxford, Berlin and Auckland. Sir imparted knowledge on discourse writing, how to write a convincing thesis statement and research paper presentation. Attendees extolled the session and showed their willingness to venture into the field of research. The session was a big hit !
