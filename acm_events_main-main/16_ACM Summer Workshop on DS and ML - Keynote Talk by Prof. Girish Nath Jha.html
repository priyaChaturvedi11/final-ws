ACM Summer Workshop on DS and ML - Keynote Talk by Prof. Girish Nath Jha

The session was conducted in the Summer Workshop - 2020. The speaker of the session was Prof. Girish Nath Jha, Professor, School of Sanskrit and Indic Studies, JNU. This was a Keynote Talk on Sanskrit and Computers. It talked about the use of Sanskrit, one of the oldest languages, in the modern field of Computers and its domains. It was an one of a kind experience for the participants to learn about the fusion of ancient and modern knowledge, which interested attendees to make it a more interactive session.
