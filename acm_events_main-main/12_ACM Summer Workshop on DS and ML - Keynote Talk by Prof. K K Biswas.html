ACM Summer Workshop on DS and ML - Keynote Talk by Prof. K K Biswas

The session was conducted in the Summer Workshop-2020. The Keynote Speaker in this session was none other than Prof. K. K. Biswas, Emeritus Professor at IIT Delhi. He addressed the topic of Generative Autoencoders. The session was quite interactive and learning. All the participants of the workshop raised some interesting questions and gave their contribution with enthusiasm making the event a success.
