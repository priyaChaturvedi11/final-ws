InfoSecGirls Meet-Delhi Chapter

This was a 3.5-hour event on 22 Feb 2020, where female tech enthusiasts were encouraged and ideated towards the field of Cybersecurity. There were multiple sessions at the event. Starting with a talk on OWASP Top 10 API (2019) – By Mr. Lalit Mohan Sharma who is an OSCP & OSWE certified security professional. Then there was a hands-on session by Mr. Chavvi Goswami - VAPT Professional at PwC and Cyber Forensics by Ms. Swati Tripathi (M. Tech. – IT (ISM) @ IGDTUW). The entire event was a success with the participation of Female Cyber Enthusiasts.
