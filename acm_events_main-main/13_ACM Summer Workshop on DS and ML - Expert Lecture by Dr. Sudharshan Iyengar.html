ACM Summer Workshop on DS and ML - Expert Lecture by Dr. Sudharshan Iyengar

The session was conducted in the Summer Workshop - 2020. The speaker was none other than Asst. Prof. Sudharshan Iyengar, IIT Ropar. He addressed students on Network Sciences. The students had one to one interaction with the Professor. The young minds shared their ideas of more engagement on Social Media from the perception of a Social Media platform. All the attendees of the workshop gave their contribution enthusiastically to make the session a success.
