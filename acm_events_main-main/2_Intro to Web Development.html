An Intro to Web Development

ACM IGDTUW organized a one-hour session on Website Development, which focused on clearing the concepts of HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap and creating a responsive website by implementing these concepts and hosting the website on Github. The session was scheduled for 13th Dec 2020 at 3 PM under the able guidance of Tanya Garg and Soumya Sharma, who are well-experienced in the domain. The session provided an amazing opportunity for students willing, to begin with, this field and hence, attracted huge participation. All participants were given certificates.
