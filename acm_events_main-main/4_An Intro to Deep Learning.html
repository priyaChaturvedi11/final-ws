An Intro to Deep Learning

ACM IGDTUW, under the Computer Science Education Week 2020 organized a one-hour session on Deep Learning and its innumerable applications on 11th December 2020 at 6-7:30 pm. This session was presided over by the subject experts- Amandeep Kaur and Muskan Lalit, who addressed the participants about the various aspects of Deep Learning, its ever-increasing demand in the industry, and its implementation using the Fast.AI library. A wide number of students participated in the session and all of them were provided with learning resources and certificates.
