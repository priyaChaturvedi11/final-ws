Learn Cybersecurity

ACM IGDTUW under the Computer Science Education Week 2020 presented a two Hour Session on "BREAKING THE WEB" on 8th December 2020. Speaker of the session, Mr. Chhavi Goswami had around 3 years of experience in cybersecurity and was working with IPCS and was looking after the web application security and infrastructure. He had also worked with PwC on projects related to the ministry of health and family welfare as part of the technical risk assurance team at Cairn-Vedanta. The session covered the details about web application based vulnerability in today's environment. Attendees got an insight about the best Cyber Security practices.
