ACM summer workshop on DS and ML - Expert lecture by Dr Niyati Baliyan

The session was conducted in the Summer Workshop - 2020. The speaker was none other than Dr. Niyati Baliyan. The session was on Semantic web.This was an interactive session and the students had one to one interaction with the Professor. A wide number of students participated in the session and all of them were provided with learning resources. The young minds shared their ideas on Semantic Web. Attendees extolled the session and showed their willingness to venture into the field of Semantic Web. The session was a big hit !
